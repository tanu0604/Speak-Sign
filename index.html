About Us
This software is designed to empower Deaf individuals by translating audio and visual content into sign language, facilitating their learning of sign language, and bridging the communication gap between Deaf and hearing communities. This platform aims to promote inclusivity and socialization, enabling seamless interactions in various social settings. Learn More

User Friendly

The website is user-friendly with an intuitive interface, easy navigation, and clear instructions. It supports English and Hindi, offers voice and text commands, and features an AI chatbot for real-time help. Visual aids and tutorials make learning ISL and using the app straightforward and accessible to all users.

Learn More

Native Language Available

The app's unique feature is its native language support, making it more accessible and setting it apart from others. The app or website is pioneering by being the first to use visuals to translate content into sign language, enhancing accessibility and communication.

Learn More

AI Bot

The AI chatbot teaches the basics of Indian Sign Language (ISL) and helps users navigate an app that converts English/Hindi audio-visual content into ISL. It offers tutorials, real-time help, and feedback on ISL practice.
